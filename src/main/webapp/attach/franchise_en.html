Partnership

If your company meet the following basic requirements, please consult the DWINS FABRIC group headquarters.

  • Administration of industry and commerce registration and business license of legitimate companies in local (content management and textiles, textile raw materials, clothing shoes and hats, and other related);
  • With local office site and sample room to meet the daily office and sample display requirements, and in accordance with the requirements of Dwins unified decoration, the area has to be more than 100 square in the local fabric market or business district store;
  • The company engaged in textile clothing or accessories business for more than 5 years, and the company's products has the same market targets with us;
  • With at least 2-3 business sales staffs.
