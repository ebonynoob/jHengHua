  • What are relationships between HengHua group and Dwins fabric?+

    Dwins fabric focus on advanced fabric. It is a subsidiary company of HengHua. HengHua is a comprehensive commercial consortium which has 30 years professional experiences of textile.

  • What kinds of business does DWINS focus on?+

    We are able to offer a lot of patterns of fabric with very adequate inventory to our customers. If you want to customize futures, please contact the nearby DWINS store for further more information.

  • What are the differences between DWINS and other fabrics wholesalers?+

    Our fabrics are all well-produced by the best quality of yarns.We never use recycled coarse yarns.

    Every pieces of our fabrics have to pass a special physical and chemical qualified test.The fabrics which do not meet qualified requirement will not be sent into all markets.

    According to our ERP system, the production of your fabric will be tracked by every procedures in detail. You can understand your fabric more clearly.

  • About tax？+

  • How to exchange and refund our fabrics?+

    Once the sales of fabric have been recorded in to ERP system, these sales can not be refunded.

    If the customers purchased wrong fabric, it is possible to exchange them for our inventory.

    If the orders have been shipped out for 7 days without receiving your exchanging requests, we will no longer apply any exchanging services for this shipment.

    If the codes of fabric lost or the packaging breaks, we will not apply exchanging services.

    If the fabric has already been cut, we will not apply exchanging services.

  • When can I get new exchanged items?+

    After receiving the goods that need to be exchanged , we will arrange the shipment for new goods in 1-2 days.

    Be attention, if your exchange or refund goods have been damaged , Dwins will ask for extra costs.

    If you have any question about order,exchanging and refunding, please feel free to contact our customer service.
