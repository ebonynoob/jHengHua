Exchanging and Refunding


  • Once the sales of fabric have been recorded in to ERP system, these sales can not be refunded.
  • If the customers purchased wrong fabric, it is possible to exchange them for our inventory .
  • If the orders have been shipped out for 7 days without receiving your exchanging requests, we will no longer apply any exchanging services for this shipment..
  • If the codes of fabric lost or the packaging breaks, we will not apply exchanging services.
  • If the fabric has already been cut, we will not apply exchanging services.

    • How to Exchange and Refund


    If you need to exchange goods, please contact the nearby Dwins Fabric store or call Dwins headquarter.

    • After receiving the goods that need to be exchanged , we will arrange the shipment for new goods in 1-2 days.
    • Be attention, if your exchange or refund goods have been damaged , Dwins will ask for extra costs.
    • If you have any question about order,exchanging and refunding, please feel free to contact our customer service.
